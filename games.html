jfdi games club

The jfdi games club is like a book club, except for video games. More specifically, it's a way to get some reason to play some of the games you've acquired in Steam but never managed to play. We'll pick a game, have two weeks to play it, and then we'll talk about the game on IRC.

If you want to join up, [subscribe to the mailing list](https://jfdi.topicbox.com/groups/games).
