jfdi club

jfdi goals club - The jfdi goals club is like a support group for people who daydream and sometimes make lists, but don't carry out their plans nearly regularly enough.

jfdi games club - The jfdi games club is like a book club, except for video games. More specifically, it's a way to get some reason to play some of the games you've acquired in Steam but never managed to play. We'll pick a game, have two weeks to play it, and then we'll talk about the game on IRC.
